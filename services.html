Brick & Block Manufacturing

We manufacture quality concrete bricks, hollow blocks and paving bricks at our own production facility in Hluvukani. All products are manufactured to South African National Standards.

Our manufacturing capability means we can supply large orders consistently and on time, with the quality control that comes from producing everything in-house.

  • Concrete bricks (various sizes)
  • Hollow blocks & maxi bricks
  • Paving bricks & pavers
  • SANS compliant products
