Our Story

Built from the Ground Up

Xiviri Trading and Projects (Pty) Ltd was founded in 2016 in Hluvukani, Bushbuckridge, Mpumalanga, with a clear vision — to provide quality construction materials and reliable project execution to communities and businesses across the region.

What started as a small sand and gravel supply operation has grown into a fully-fledged construction and supply company, with our own fleet of heavy-duty trucks, excavators, TLBs and brick manufacturing facilities.

We are proudly South African, BEE compliant, and deeply rooted in the communities we serve. Every project we take on is a commitment to quality, reliability and local economic development.

Our Journey

Company Timeline

2016

Company Founded

Xiviri Trading and Projects (Pty) Ltd is registered and begins operations supplying sand and gravel in Hluvukani.

2018

Fleet Expansion

Invested in our first fleet of dump trucks, enabling us to provide full material haulage services to clients across Bushbuckridge.

2020

Brick Manufacturing Launched

Opened our own brick and block manufacturing facility, producing concrete bricks, hollow blocks and paving bricks on site.

2022

Heavy Machinery Acquired

Added CAT excavators and TLBs to our fleet, expanding our capability into full earthworks and excavation contracts.

2024

Growing Strong

Operating 10+ vehicles and machines with a team of 30+ dedicated professionals, serving clients across Mpumalanga and beyond.
